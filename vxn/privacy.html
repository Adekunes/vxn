Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our services, or contact us for support.

Account Information

When you create an account or use our services, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and company information
  • Payment and billing information
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use our services:

  • Log data and device information
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Performance data and error reports
  • IP address and location data

Customer Data

When you use our CRM services, you may upload customer information. We process this data as your data processor, following your instructions and our data processing agreement.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and secure our services:

Service Delivery

  • Providing and maintaining our CRM platform
  • Processing transactions and managing accounts
  • Delivering customer support and communications
  • Personalizing your experience

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
  • Developing new products and services
  • Conducting research and analytics
  • Training our AI models (with appropriate safeguards)

Security & Compliance

  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations
  • Protecting against security threats
  • Maintaining service reliability

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our services:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Customer support tools (Zendesk, Intercom)
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • To comply with legal processes or government requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To investigate potential violations of our terms
  • To prevent fraud or security threats

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • 24/7 monitoring and threat detection

Organizational Measures

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Strict access controls and background checks
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular security assessments and updates

Compliance Certifications

We maintain industry-standard certifications including:

  • ISO 27001 (Information Security Management)
  • SOC 2 Type II (Security, Availability, Confidentiality)
  • GDPR compliance for EU data subjects
  • CCPA compliance for California residents

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Access & Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Know what information we have about you
  • Understand how we use your information

Correction & Deletion

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances

Data Processing Controls

  • Withdraw consent for processing
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities
  • Exercise rights through our privacy portal

To exercise your rights: Contact us at privacy@vxn-vision.com or use our contact form.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze service usage:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and performance
  • Essential service functionality

Analytics & Performance

We use analytics to understand how our services are used:

  • Google Analytics for website usage insights
  • Performance monitoring and error tracking
  • User experience research and optimization
  • Service reliability and uptime monitoring

Marketing & Personalization

These cookies help us provide relevant content and features:

  • Personalized recommendations and content
  • Marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Feature usage and adoption tracking
  • Customer journey optimization

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent manager.

International Transfers

VXN VISION operates globally, and your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own:

Data Processing Locations

We primarily process data in the following locations:

  • United States (primary data center)
  • European Union (GDPR-compliant processing)
  • Canada (adequate protection recognized)
  • Other locations as needed for service delivery

Transfer Safeguards

When transferring data internationally, we ensure adequate protection:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Binding corporate rules for internal transfers
  • Additional technical and organizational safeguards

Your Rights

Regardless of where your data is processed, you retain all rights under applicable privacy laws, and we maintain the same level of protection.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16 years of age:

Age Restrictions

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16
  • Our services require users to be at least 18 years old
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us
  • We will promptly delete any such information upon verification

Educational Use

For educational institutions using our services:

  • We comply with applicable educational privacy laws (FERPA, COPPA)
  • Educational data is processed under separate agreements
  • Additional safeguards apply to student information
  • Contact us for educational institution-specific privacy information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws:

Notification Process

  • We will notify you of material changes via email
  • Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date
  • Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of changes
  • You may request deletion of your data if you disagree with changes

Change Categories

We may update this policy for various reasons:

  • New features or services we offer
  • Changes in applicable laws or regulations
  • Improvements to our privacy practices
  • Business operations or structure changes
